Roles and responsibilities

When Clara runs as part of your monday.com automation, monday.com workspace administrators and their organisations typically decide why and how board data is processed in their accounts. Quinsy processes cell values only to perform the formatting action you configured and acts as a data processor for that automation processing. monday.com remains the platform provider through which automations are triggered, authenticated, and billed.

What we process

When your monday.com automation runs, we receive and process the following data sent by monday:

  • Board ID (boardId)
  • Item ID (itemId)
  • Column ID (columnId)
  • Cell content (columnValue)
  • Selected format type (formatType)
  • Account, workspace, and user identifiers included in monday.com automation payloads (such as account or user IDs needed to execute the action)

We use short-lived monday API tokens provided during each automation run to read the current cell value and write back the formatted result.

Website visitors

When you visit clara.quinsy.app, our hosting provider processes technical data required to deliver the site, including IP address, browser type, requested pages, timestamps, and referrer information. We use this data to operate, secure, and troubleshoot the website. We do not use this information for advertising profiles.

What we do not do

  • We do not store cell content persistently.
  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not use your data for advertising.
  • We do not build user profiles from board data.

Legal basis

We process data on the following bases under the GDPR:

  • Contract (Art. 6(1)(b)): to deliver the formatting automation you configured.
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)): to operate, secure, and improve Clara, prevent abuse, enforce plan limits, and maintain service reliability. You may object to processing based on legitimate interests where applicable.

Sub-processors

  • Vercel (hosting): our API and website run on Vercel infrastructure.
  • monday.com (platform): sends automation payloads, provides API access for read/write operations, and handles marketplace billing and metering where applicable.

International transfers

Some sub-processors may process data outside the European Economic Area, including in the United States. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses and supplementary measures offered by our providers.

Retention

Cell data is processed in real time during each automation run and is not retained in our application database. Server logs on Vercel may temporarily contain request metadata. Log retention follows Vercel's default policies (typically up to 30 days for runtime logs).

Usage and billing

Clara tracks how many automation transformations you use through monday.com's app subscription API. We do not maintain a separate usage database or store cell content for metering. Usage limits depend on your monday marketplace plan. Payment and invoicing are handled by monday.com.

Children

Clara is a business tool for monday.com workspaces and is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
