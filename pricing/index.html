What counts as a transformation? One successful Clara automation run — including when the loop guard skips a write because the formatted value is already correct.

How billing works

You choose or change your plan in the monday.com marketplace when you install Clara or from Installed Apps in your monday account. monday.com handles payment and invoicing. Clara enforces your plan's monthly transformation limit in the automation service.

If you reach your monthly limit, automations return an error until the next billing period or you upgrade your plan.
